About GROCEMAC

  2. About GROCEMAC

Aiming for a green future with sustainable energy being accessible to everyone, GROCEMAC was founded and based in Nigeria. Inevitably, soaring energy prices make it more lucrative to switch to renewable energy and benefit from our infinite energy resource; the sun. We at GROCEMAC share a common passion which actively supports the energy transition for a clean future.

Founders & Team

Solar energy is by no doubt gaining prominence these days considering the shift towards sustainable solutions in all fields. The idea of solar energy bloomed in our company after 16 years of dedication in the manufacturing industry and deeply connecting with consumers' needs. Therefore, we built brands founded on trust, loyalty and professionalism.

Green Power for All
